The Attune Loop

A simple, repeatable feedback process for regulating reactions in real time.

Step 1 Notice

Observe what is happening in your body and your attention. Catch the reaction before it becomes a spiral.

Step 2 Name

Put a precise label on your current state. Naming an emotion engages the prefrontal cortex and can dampen reactivity.

Step 3 Adjust

Choose a small, specific action to shift your state. This is not about suppressing, but skillfully navigating.

Step 4 Recover

Return toward your baseline. Reflect briefly on the rep to build the neural pathway for next time.

Why loops work

The Attune Loop leverages feedback mechanics and state-dependent learning. By repeatedly cycling through observation, labeling, and micros-adjustments, you train your nervous system to self-regulate more automatically over time. It transforms abstract concepts of "being calm" into a concrete, executable process.
