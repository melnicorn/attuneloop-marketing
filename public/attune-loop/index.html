The Attune Loop

A simple, repeatable feedback process for regulating reactions in real time.

Step 1 Notice

Observe whatâ€™s happening in your body and attention. Awareness is the first step to regulation.

Step 2 Name

Apply a precise label to your current state. Naming an emotion can help reduce its intensity.

Step 3 Adjust

Choose a small, specific action to shift your state. This builds agency and flexibility.

Step 4 Recover

Return toward baseline and learn from the repetition. Recovery strengthens your resilience over time.

Why loops work

The Attune Loop is based on the principles of feedback, repetition, and state-dependent learning. By repeatedly cycling through these steps, you train your nervous system to recognize and regulate states more automatically.

It avoids complex theories in favor of direct, actionable feedback loops that you can practice anywhere.
